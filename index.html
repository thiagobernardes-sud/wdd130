Maranhão

Maranhão is a state located in the northeast of Brazil. Situated at southeast of the Amazon River basin, it has a great number of rivers supporting farming and cattle raising.It is also there that you will find the formation of dunes known as lençóis maranhenses, one of the most visited points in the state.
